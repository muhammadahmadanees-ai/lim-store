Our standard tile sizes are 30×30 cm, 60×60 cm, and 60×120 cm. We also offer fully custom sizes for bespoke projects — contact us to discuss your requirements.

Terrazzo tiles should be installed by a professional using a suitable adhesive for stone tiles. Ensure the subfloor is clean, flat, and dry. Grout joints of 1.5–2 mm are recommended. After installation, seal the surface with a penetrating stone sealer.

Use a pH-neutral cleaner and a damp mop for daily cleaning — avoid acidic cleaners like vinegar as they can etch the surface. Re-seal every 1–2 years depending on traffic. For deep cleaning, use a specialist stone cleaner.
